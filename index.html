Want to work for the Mouse?

It's every kids dream to go to Disney World! Now, what if you were able to create the magic you experienced as a kid? The Disney College Program offers active college students the experience of a life time! Through the Disney College Program, students can work in one of the several famous Walt Disney World Resorts or in one of the six reknowned theme parks: The Magic Kingdom, Disney's Animal Kingdom, EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, Blizzard Beach, and Typhoon Lagoon.

Cast member roles range from Character Performer, Quick Service Food and Beverage, Attractions, Custodial, Parade and Audience Control, Merchandise, Vacation Planner, Costumes, and Lifeguard! The Walt Disney Company will carefully evaluate you to place you in the role that you can make the most magic for guests from all over the world!

How to Apply:

Go to Disney College Program applications and start your journey to become a Walt Disney World Cast Member! You'll encounter a web-based interview, which if all goes well will lead to a phone interview! Once you've completed these steps, anxiously await your acceptance email from the Mouse!
